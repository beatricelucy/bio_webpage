Hello. I’m Bea.

I like building things. Sometimes products. But mainly teams and ideas.

At the moment I’m Good Things Foundation’s Director of Design and Explorations where I set up a social design studio.

We’re a multidisciplinary team discovering new ways to tackle some of society’s most entrenched problems. We do this by working closely with people and communities. We don’t pretend to have the answers and we experiment with new ways to find the great stuff that’s already happening out there in the real world.
